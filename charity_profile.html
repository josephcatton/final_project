Charity Profiles

Feeding America

The largest hunger-relief organization in the U.S., providing food through a network of food banks.

Feeding America partners with over 200 food banks across the U.S.

Habitat for Humanity

Helps build affordable housing for low-income families.

Habitat builds homes using volunteer labor and donations.

World Wildlife Fund (WWF)

Dedicated to global conservation efforts, protecting endangered species and promoting sustainability.

WWF works in over 100 countries on conservation efforts.

American Red Cross

Provides disaster relief, emergency assistance, and blood donation services.

The Red Cross supports communities during crises globally.
